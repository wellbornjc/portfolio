Dungeon Camp
Dungeon Camp is a precision platforming game developed to learn the Lua code language and Love2D game engine. Help Pops carry his tent through a variety of platforming challenges and be the first to journey to the end of The Abandoned Dungeon to setup for the most remote camping trip! I designed and developed the game and created all art assets, sound effects, and music as well as managed the marketing and social media. Dungeon Camp launched on Steam and Itch.io.

Sorcery Scramble
Sorcery Scramble is a high score based arcade game developed in a week as a self imposed challange to teach myself the Pico-8 game engine. Do The Sorcery Scramble! Dodge fireballs to stay alive. Drink dash potions to teleport and become invincible for a bit. Gather hearts to gain more lives. Gather coins to spend at Rondo's Rat Shop. I designed and developed the game as well as created all art assets, sound effects, and music. Sorcery Scramble launched on Itch.io and can be played in your web browser.

Doodle Box
A simple pixel art doodling tool written in JavaScript. The idea for Doodle Box came from my need for simple custom icons for different projects. You can choose between different size square canvases. Once you've completed your doodle, you can download it to use in any project.
